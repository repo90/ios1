NoBlur

  • Supports iOS 7.0 to 12.1.2

Description

  • NoBlur speeds up your device by removing blur effects on the SpringBoard and in any application!

    The difference is most noticable on older devices (iPhone 4, 4s, 5, iPad 2, Air, etc.) and devices that are struggling to keep up with the latest iOS release.

    There are no options to configure, just install and go!
